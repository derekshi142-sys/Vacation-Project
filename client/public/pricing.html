Frequently Asked Questions

How is the percentage calculated?

The fee is calculated based on your total trip budget including flights, accommodation, food, and activities. We only charge when you're completely satisfied with your itinerary.

What if I need to change my plans?

Standard plans include one major revision. Premium plans include unlimited revisions and real-time adjustments during your trip.

Do you charge for cancellations?

No cancellation fees! If you cancel before we start booking, you only pay for the planning service already provided.

How does the booking service work?

We use your credit card to book directly with airlines, hotels, and activity providers. You get all confirmation details and can manage bookings yourself.

What about the referral program?

Refer someone who books a trip over $1,000 and get $100 credit towards your next vacation. No limits on referrals!
